The Future of music




Recently some improve in the making of music have been made, we make music more easily with computers, anybody can create music, but the music has always followed the evolution of the world, and its pretty hard to say what's going to happened in the future. Some people tried to do it, but it's mostly fictional, and we can't really take that for sure. For exemple Lucio is a fictional DJ in the years 2070's and they made an album for him, clic on the image on right to listen to it.



<



But with the technology evolving, Artificial intelligence is starting to be able to create music, some algorithm are able to understand what makes music, the rythm, the notes that follow each others, and are able to create new stuff with it. If you click on the picture on your left, you'll listen to Daddy's Car, a music totally made by an AI in 2016 with the style of the beatles, try to imagine what is possible right now, and what will be in 20 years.
