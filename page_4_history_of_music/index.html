history of music

Music can be dated back as early as 35,000 years ago. They found significant ways to create music with hand made instruments. For example they would make flutes out of animal bones. It's also believed that precussions instruments were the most commonly used because of how available they were, such as using rocks or sticks as drums.





Religion played a major part in early music. The church was the main source of publuc music during this time period. Although the voice was the main instrument during this time, there were also wind instruments and percussions that have became more advanced throughout the years.

During the Renessiance period music becomes more advenced and modernized. Music becomes more than just religion and composers expand their talents.wood instruments become more popular and because of printing notated music sheets become more accessable.

Classical music was said to be originated in ancient greek but wasn't popularized until the 1700's. The orchestra starts to become its own such as the string quartet and symphony. During this time oprah and choral music come to play as well. Instruments as well start to become more modernized to what we know today.

During the 1800's romantic music began to rise and classical music began to fade away. The music in this time period was dramatic, expressionate, poetic, and would tell stories. During this time aswell the quality and range of the instuments we know today became widely improved, especially ones that were more expressive.

The 20th century is on the rise and music becomes more diversed. Music during this time period is more responsive to political opression and conflict. Cinimas start offering composers more opportunities as new forms of music begins. As technology also advances during this time and is competing against and with music andvancing into the modern music we know today.
